AQUES Training

Training Objective

This training aims to equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively use the AQUES system, deliver structured lessons, and execute materials proficiently. Participants will be assessed on lesson flow, communication, and overall teaching performance.

Part 1: Rules

This set of guidelines helps supporters maintain a professional and effective environment during AQUES lessons.

Part 2: Lesson Flow

This guide shows how to conduct the mock demo, reflecting an actual AQUES lesson, aligning with student expectations, and demonstrating the structured flow of the lesson.

Pronunciation

This training helps Japanese learners practice pronunciation, the linking and dropping of sounds, and the natural rhythm of English to improve their speaking and listening skills.

Translation Exercise

In this exercise, students learn English through set phrases and expressions. With continuous repetition, they build vocabulary, fluency, and conversational skills

ACP Foundations

This training is designed to help students describe pictures, learn new vocabulary, and improve their conversational skills.
