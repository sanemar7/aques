AQUES Training

Introduction to AQUES

AQUES is a Japanese online English conversation platform offering a unique hybrid learning experience led by both a Japanese concierge and a supporter.


To support progress, custom materials are provided to practice pronunciation, grammar, and conversation skills, making each lesson both engaging and effective.


With zero preparation or review required, learners enjoy efficient, stress-free lessons tailored to real-world speaking skills. All lessons are one-on-one, and AQUES is popular among adult learners aged 18 to 75, helping them speak English confidently without ever leaving Japan.

Training Objective

This training aims to equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively use the AQUES system, deliver structured lessons, and execute materials proficiently. Participants will be assessed on lesson flow, communication, and overall teaching performance.
